LSF Level V's

The highest achievement in the League of Silent Flight is the attainment of LEVEL V in the Soaring Accomplishment Program. Level V requires completion of the following tasks:

  • A thermal duration flight of at least two hours.
  • A slope flight of at least eight hours.
  • A goal and return flight of at least ten kilometers.
  • Three WINS and 12,000 points in at least six contests with twenty or more unique contestants.

Over 130 members of LSF have completed these challenging tasks and are known and respected worldwide as outstanding RC model sailplane pilots. A plaque commemorating their achievement is on permanent display in the lobby of the National Model Aviation Museum at the Academy of Model Aeronautics in Muncie, Indiana.
